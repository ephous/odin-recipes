Pancakes

Description

Make these delicious pancakes this weekend with our simple, classic recipe!

Top with fresh fruit, butter and/or maple syrup to make a perfect breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1-1.2 cups flour
  • 3T sugar
  • 1-3/4t baking powder
  • 1/4t salt
  • 3T unsalted butter
  • one large egg
  • 1c whole milk

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients and mix well
  2. Whisk egg and then mix in milk
  3. Melt butter and quickly add to milk-n-egg mixture
  4. Pour mixture into dry ingredients and stir until blended
  5. Pour pancake batter into 1/4c circle on griddle at 375F
  6. Once bubbles appear in pancake, flip and finish
  7. Serve immediately with choice of topping!
